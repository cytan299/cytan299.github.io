Field DeRotator

The field derotator was my first project. My main reason for designing and building a field derotator is because I like alt-az mounts and really, really dislike equatorial mounts. My research into field derotators uncovered a common myth among hobbyists that they are difficult to use. Fortunately, after successfully building and then using my field derotator, I believe I have debunked this myth completely. See for example, the Crab Nebula that I took using my field derotator.
